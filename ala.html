HOW DO WE SAVE THEM?

Go Vegan!
Going vegan means that you stop eating, wearing, or using anything that came from an animal, such as meat, milk, eggs, and leather. Just by going vegan, you'll save the lives of nearly 200 animals a year. Pretty awesome, right?

Cut Out Dissection
If your class is planning to dissect animals, ask your teacher for a humane activity instead, such as using a computer program to perform a virtual dissection. An animal will be saved, and you'll learn and remember way more.
